# MonkePhone:
MonkePhone is a utility mod for Gorilla Tag founded by [Striker](https://github.com/Striker-67) and [Biotest](https://github.com/biotest052) with development led by [Dev](https://github.com/developer9998). Although it is often regarded as an overrated photo capture mod, in reality its not just that.

All edits to this has nothing to do with any developers listed above. All edits are solely from the contributors to this fork.
If you want the original version of MonkePhone it can be located [here](https://github.com/developer9998/MonkePhone).

## Whats this fork for?
I am planning on adding a bunch more content to MonkePhone as dev9998 has publicly stated there disliking towards this mod and i dont see much/any progress being made by them in the future.

## MonkePhone Memories:
These following photos are from early MonkePhone development. They show off how features have changed and advanced throughout the development of this mod and showcase how much effort has been put into this mod up to the point shown. Please bear in mind that while viewing the following content none of the contributors to this fork have access to the Google Drive all images posted are solely the responsibility of the uploader [Dev](https://github.com/developer9998).

MonkePhone's development archive: [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/15R_B7z_QMueqlCNcDCqLoZGEu5QFAk59?usp=sharing)

On the Google Drive it consists of two different major parts of development and something deeper.
- Firstly it has all photos taken and uploaded to the testing server at the start of 2024.
- All photos posted in the publicly visiable "MonkeGram" channel in dev9998's discord server.
- Lastly, Dev + Striker and Bio havent just created a mod. Over the vast time from the start of 2024 to the end of 2025 they have given more people access to post these photos to what you can view on the Google Drive. They have met new people and had experiences which they will never forget.
